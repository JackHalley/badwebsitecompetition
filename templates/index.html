About the Competition

Have you ever come across a website so confusing, so weirdly designed, or just plain 'bad' that it's actually good? This is the place to showcase those unique gems! Whether it's a blast from the past, an odd design choice, or a navigation nightmare, we want to see it. Join the fun, submit an entry, or just enjoy the gallery of web design wonders.

How It Works

  1. Find & Submit: Stumble upon a weird website? Submit the link!
  2. Vote: Every month, vote for your favorite 'bad' website.
  3. Win: The top-voted site wins the title of "Worst Website of the Month" and a fun, quirky prize!
